# OurHeritage üé®

OurHeritage is a comprehensive cultural social platform that bridges the gap between handcraft enthusiasts and skilled artisans. It enables users to discover, connect with, and support traditional handcrafters through content sharing, real-time communication, and interactive engagement. The platform is built to preserve and promote cultural heritage by creating a vibrant community where artisans can showcase their work, users can explore authentic handcrafted products, and meaningful connections are formed through a shared appreciation of traditional craftsmanship.

### Social Networking
- **User Profiles**: Comprehensive profiles for both handcrafters and enthusiasts

- **Content Sharing**: Share posts, photos, and updates about handcrafted items  
- **Engagement**: Like and comment on posts to interact with the community

### Real-Time Communication
- **Private Messaging**: One-on-one conversations with other users  
- **Group Chats**: Create and join public or private group discussions

  - New likes on your content
  - Comments on your posts
  - New followers
  - Incoming messages  
- **SignalR Integration**: Seamless real-time bidirectional communication  

---

### Advanced Search & Discovery
- **Smart Search System**: Find handcrafters based on:
